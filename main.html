About Andok's

Since 1985, Andok's has been serving the Philippines with the most delicious and affordable roasted chicken. Our secret recipe and traditional roasting methods have made us the go-to choice for Filipino families.

We pride ourselves on using only the freshest ingredients and maintaining the highest quality standards. Every chicken is carefully selected and roasted to perfection, ensuring that golden, crispy skin and juicy, flavorful meat that our customers love.

With over 35 years of experience, Andok's has become a household name synonymous with quality, taste, and value.
